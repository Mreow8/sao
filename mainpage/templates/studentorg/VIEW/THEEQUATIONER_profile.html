THE EQUATIONERS LOGO SYMBOLISM

In the realm of engineering, symbolism can often be found embedded within the tools, colors, and concepts that shape the profession. From the authoritative hue of blue to the practicality symbolized by a yellow hard hat, each element carries layers of meaning that resonate within the engineering community. Let's delve into these symbols, unraveling their significance in the context of trust, innovation, and optimism that define the engineer's world.

Key elements of the logo include:

  • Blue - denotes authority, trust, and confidence. It also associated with freedom, intuition, imagination, inspiration, and sensitivity in which an engineer should possess.
  • Yellow - if associated with construction. It is easy to see even in challenging conditions.
  • White - symbolizes softness and cleanliness because even though engineers are often viewed as serious and intimidating, they also possess these kinds of characters.
  • System Symbol - as an Industrial Engineering, it requires an organization of ideas and innovations to be shared with everyone.
  • Yellow Hard Hat - represents a thinking that you looked at issues in the most positive light possible.
