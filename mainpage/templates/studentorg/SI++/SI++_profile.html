SI++ LOGO SYMBOLISM

Evolving Futures, Igniting Innovation: FSTLP's Ratified Logo Sparks a New Era in Science and Technology Leadership!

The Future Science and Technology Leaders of the Philippines (FSTLP) unveils its new logo, symbolizing progress and excellence. This dynamic design reflects the organization's commitment to shaping future leaders in science and technology.

View CBL Register Officer List of Officers Register Adviser List Adviser

Key elements of the logo include:

  • Burning Torch: Represents the relentless pursuit of innovation in science, research, and technology.
  • Gears: Symbolize unity and visionary progress within the group.
  • Laurel Leaves: Stand for wisdom, honor, growth, friendship, renewal, and opportunities.
  • Scale: Emphasizes the balance between academic responsibilities and commitment to FSTLP.
  • Open Book: Reflects shared knowledge and respect for the Constitution and Bylaws.
  • Computer and Microscope: Represent the core fields of science and technology.
  • Silhouette of Progression: Signifies the commitment to advancing FSTLP's vision, mission, and goals.

The new logo embodies FSTLP's dedication to fostering curiosity, knowledge, and leadership in the ever-evolving realm of science and technology.
