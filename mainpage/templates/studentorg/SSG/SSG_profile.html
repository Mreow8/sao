SSG LOGO SYMBOLISM

The Supreme Student Government (SSG) of Cebu Technological University - Argao Campus has a mission to protect and defend the rights and responsibilities of students. At the same time, it aims to serve as a guiding light and promote unity and cooperation among the university community.

To represent this mission, the SSG has a logo that features a shield with a torch in the center. The torch symbolizes the pursuit of knowledge, enlightenment, and leadership, while the shield represents protection and defense. This signifies the SSG's commitment to promoting a culture of collaboration and togetherness among all university community members.

The logo communicates the SSG's commitment to empowering and advocating for students while promoting a learning, leadership, and collaboration culture. Through its various initiatives and programs, the SSG ensures students can access the resources they need to succeed and thrive academically and personally.

Key elements of the logo include:

  • Hand Holding the Light: Emphasizes the SSG's commitment to excellence and leadership.
  • Face: Represents the student body and their diverse perspectives.
  • Symbol of Justice: Reflects the SSG's dedication to fairness and equity.
  • Leaf: Symbolizes growth and development.
  • Star: Represents excellence, signifying the SSG's goal of encouraging students to strive for their best and achieve their full potential.

The new logo embodies the SSG's dedication to fostering curiosity, knowledge, and leadership in the ever-evolving realm of science and technology.
